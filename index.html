Features

Whales with no loyalty to the project are not a problem with Super Bear. A max transaction limit on selling prevents whales from dumping the token and dropping its price. Buys, on the other hand, have no limit.

3% HOLDR Rewards

HOLDR rewards are similar to dividends in the stock market. A 3% fee charged on all non-buy transactions allows the reward balance to accumulate. The result gets distributed to all HOLDRs.

3% Automatic Liquidity

As the liquidity pool for Super Bear grows, so does its liquidity and its hedge against price fluctuations. Equipped with a 3% transaction fee on all non-buy transactions, the pool for Super Bear can always keep growing.

3% Burn

Super Bear burns tokens regularly and automatically to maintain its deflationary status. All non-buy transactions get charged a 3% fee. The accumulated tokens are removed from the total supply forever.

3% Marketing

As great as Super Bear can be, it cannot grow without promotion. For that reason, all non-buy transactions get charged another 3% fee for marketing Super Bear to grow our community.

FAQ

What is Super Bear Token?
Super Bear is a deflationary token on the Binance Smart Chain. Its features include automatic liquidity for price stabilization, HOLDR Rewards, burning to facilitate deflation, and a Marketing Wallet to promote the project for growth. A max transaction limit is also in place, preventing whales from selling at mass and dropping the token price.
What Fees Does Super Bear Charge?
There are four fees but, buyers get charged none of them. The fees are 3% for Automatic Liquidity, 3% for HOLDR Rewards, 3% for the Marketing Wallet and 3% for Burning. All these fees are automatic, allowing Super Bear to operate with no human intervention.
What is Automatic Liquidity?
For traders to be able to trade a token, the token needs a pool. The larger a token's pool balance is, the safer the hedge against price volatility or severe price drops. Super Bear charges a 3% fee for liquidity which gets deposited automatically into the PancakeSwap pool, ensuring the safety of the token.
What are HOLDR Rewards?
Similar to dividends, HOLDR rewards get automatically sent by the Super Bear contract to all HOLDRs with Super Bear in their wallets. The more you HOLD, the larger your reward is. The funds rewarded come from the 3% HOLDR Rewards fee charged at transactions.
What is The Marketing Wallet?
For Super Bear to grow, our team needs funds to promote it. These funds get collected from the 3% Marketing Wallet fee charged at transactions and sent to the Marketing Wallet where the team can access it.
What is the Max Transaction Limit?
When many people sell a token at once, its demand decreases and thus, so does its price. The Max Transaction Limit is in place to prevent whales (people with lots of money) from selling too much too fast, preventing nasty price drops.
